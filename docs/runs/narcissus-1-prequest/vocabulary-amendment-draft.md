**Status: DRAFT / PROPOSAL. Not applied.** Widening a registered mythological term is a deliberate edit to

## Current registered meaning
> **Narcissus** — iterative UI rendering and visual review through repeated reflection.

## Proposed registered meaning
> **Narcissus** — production of full-rollout, award-standard front-ends for enrolled products through the
> reflection loop: iterative UI rendering and visual review, repeated until the surface is genuinely
> beautiful, distinctive, and functionally complete.

## What changes, precisely
The current meaning names a **review/reflection loop only** (render → look → refine). The vision EXTENDS it to
a **front-end PRODUCTION capability**: Narcissus does not merely critique UI, it *produces* finished,
award-standard front-ends — with the reflection loop as its engine, not its whole scope.

## Rationale (myth-faithful)
Narcissus was transfixed by his own reflection. The engine is therefore the **reflection loop** (render → look
hard → refine → look again), run until the surface is worthy. The myth already implies the loop; the amendment
makes explicit that the loop's *output* is a produced front-end, not just a verdict.

## Scope guards (must remain true; not part of the registered string, but binding)
- Narcissus produces front-ends for the enrolled **products** (`ai-forge` / `forge` / `saas-forge`), NOT for
  TELOS's zero-dependency governance core (which stays framework-free). It gives the products it governs
  surfaces worthy of the rigor beneath them.
- "Award-standard" is the **default bar reached for**, not an aspiration — Awwwards-Site-of-the-Year tier.
- The reflection loop terminates only when a **two-blade gate** passes: (1) aesthetic — Eye-judged human
  review (beauty has no oracle); (2) functional — a deterministic E2E test asserting every interactive
  element works. "Good enough" fails both.
